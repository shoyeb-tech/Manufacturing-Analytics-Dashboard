# Manufacturing-Analytics-Dashboard

Manufacturing Analytics Dashboard

The purpose of this dashboard is to monitor manufacturing performance by analyzing production, processed, and rejected quantities. It helps identify inefficiencies, improve quality, and support data-driven decision-making.

# Tech Stack
.Power BI – Dashboard creation & data visualization

# DATA SOURCE
.Data provided by ExcelR.

# Business Problem
-The company faced several challenges:
.Manual reporting using Excel leading to errors
.Lack of real-time production insights
.Difficulty in tracking rejection trends
.No clear visibility of machine and employee performance

# Goal of the Dashboard
.To provide a centralized and interactive dashboard that tracks production efficiency, identifies rejection patterns, and helps in      improving overall operational performance.

# KPI Cards
.Displays total produced quantity (60.05M), processed quantity (60.02M), and rejected quantity (491K) to give a quick performance       overview.

.Production Over Time (Line Chart)
 Shows quarterly production trends, highlighting a dip in Q3 and recovery in Q4.
 
.Rejected Quantity by Machine (Bar Chart)
 Identifies machines contributing the most to defects, helping in maintenance decisions.
 
.Department-wise Manufacture vs Rejected (column Chart)
 Compares production and rejection across departments to detect inefficiencies.
 
.Manufacture vs Rejected (Pie Chart)
 Shows overall quality ratio with ~99% successful production and ~1% rejection.
 
.Rejected Quantity by Employee (column Chart)
 Tracks employee-level rejection to support performance monitoring and training.

# Filters (Slicers)
  Includes Month, Employee, and Department filters for detailed analysis.

# Business Impact & Insights
.Production efficiency is above 99%, indicating strong performance
.A few machines contribute to the majority of rejections
.Employee-level insights help in improving workforce performance
.Production dip in Q3 highlights potential operational issues
.Enables faster and more accurate decision-making
